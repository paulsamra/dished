Frequently Asked Questions


What is Dished™?

Dished is a social app built on the iOS platform to help you discover, share and rate amazing food and drinks around you.

Where are you located?

Dished is proudly being launched in San Francisco, however Dished will work at any restaurant across the United States upon our launch.

Where does the name come from?

“Dish” means a couple things. First and most obvious being a serving vessel that your favorite meals are served on. We like Dish used as a verb, meaning to discuss or to forcefully share your feelings. That is what Dished is as its core, a place for you to discuss your thoughts about the food and drinks you enjoy at restaurants with your friends and family.

How did the idea come about?

Our founder, Will, went to a new restaurant that everyone he trusted was raving about. It was well reviewed by both critics and everyday people online. He made a reservation and couldn’t wait to try it! Friday finally arrived and everything was great, except for the ceviche he ordered. It was, well, without turning your stomach with the details…not so great. Although a bit scarred by the experience, he gave the restaurant another chance and learned after a couple more trips that THE dish to order at this restaurant was the shrimp taco plate. He would have rated the ceviche an F, the shrimp tacos an A+! That is where the idea came about, creating an app where you can always know not only what restaurants to eat at based on reviews from those you trust, but more importantly, what dishes to order!

How is Dished all that different?

We love food and we love online reviews for restaurants! They are great tools for discovering new places. The problem we found was that restaurant review websites take into consideration many different factors, the food, the decor, the service, and the ambiance. We focus 100% on the food. When we are craving a specific dish and are on the lookout for the best Carne Asada Burrito, we don’t want to see the best rated Mexican restaurant, we want to see the best rated Carne Asada Burrito. Dished celebrates the dishes that keep us returning over and over again to our favorite places.
  1. Ever been to a restaurant and loved a dish but couldn’t remember what is was 6 months later? Or been to a restaurant you love, but ordered the wrong thing? Your personal stream will keep all of your reviews organized so you can have your own personalized food guide.
  2. Trust your circle. We don’t know about you, but we rely on our friends and family to give us the best advice on where to find the best food. That is why Dished is 100% made for sharing and comparing food within your own circle. When you search for a dish, say Vegetarian pizza, the top rated dishes will display in your area. We take things even further and have dishes reviewed by your friends and family display first, so you are always getting the best advice possible. Grandma may have great taste in food, but she is no Bobby Flay, that is why Dished will be granting our power users and movers and shakers in the industry, “Influencer Status”. Like with your friends, reviews left by influencers will always display first on your search results so you are getting advise from the best in the biz!
  3. Share. Tag. Hashtag. Easily comment on reviews, share your thoughts on the reviews of your friends, tag your friends, and #telltheworldwhatyouthink!
  4. Prevent fake reviews and celebrate consistency. We require you to take a picture of the dish you are reviewing, so that we know those leaving a review have actually eaten the dish. This also will also let others see whether or not the dish is consistently cooked and prepared. We do not allow restaurants to leave reviews on their own dishes. We think that would be pretty unfair. We do, however, let restaurants claim their dishes and add additional information about dishes such as the preparation and where the ingredients come from. We also let restaurants respond to your reviews in comments so that communication between restaurant and patron is easy!
  5. We know the best food doesn’t always come from a fancy restaurant. Our app is food-focused so we give all kinds of food sources, from farmer’s market stands to food trucks the ability to have an equal footing.

How is Dished social?

Dished allows you to follow other users within the application. If you sign up with Facebook in Dished or permit us access to your contacts list upon downloading the app, we will automatically follow your friends and contacts that have already downloaded Dished on your behalf so you don’t have to manually make connections. Your homefeed shows all the recent reviews left by those you follow to keep you up to speed on what you friends and family are reviewing.

You can comment and @tag your friends in your own reviews or on the reviews of others to keep the conversation going.

You can YUM a review by either clicking the YUM button or by double-clicking on the photo itself on the homefeed.

Your “news” feed will keep you up to date on all the social happenings in the app, whether on your own dishes/reviews or on those of people you follow.

Share posts to other social networks and beyond. Dished allows you to share your reviews or to posts you enjoy to Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Tumblr, and via email.

How do I search for Food or Drinks in the app?

Searching for food and drinks has never been easier with Dished. Once logged into Dished, head to the Explore Tab. From here you can search in a number of ways.

Search Bar: search by Dish Title, #hashtag, @username, Restaurant name

See the top rated dishes by title: Search by keyword tittle (type a keyword and dishes with the same keyword in the title will display, ex. Cheeseburger). You can also enter multiple keywords or a string of keywords such as Vegan or Vegan Pizza or Vegan Pepperoni Pizza.

See the top rated dishes by category: Search by hashtag (typing # then the hashtag that you are interested in seeing results for ex. #brunch). You can also click on any blue hashtag on a review page or in a comment and get instant search results.

See dishes your friends have reviewed: Search by username (typing @ then the user’s name in the search bar).

See the top rated dishes at any restaurant: Start typing the name of the restaurant and select it from the auto-recommendation list. Once on the restaurant profile, you can see what dishes have been reviewed by other users.

Use our recommendations: We provide you three sections on our explore tab to help you find great dishes around you.

Editor’s Pick: These are Dishes and drinks that the Dished team in your city is loving at the moment. This rotating list should keep you up to speed on what we think are some of the best dishes in your area.

Popular Now: In this section, Dished works its magic and show you what the most popular and trending Dishes are in your area.

Hashtag Grid: Quick search by our most popular hashtags in the system. This grid will rotate based on how you are all using Dished.

What do these icons mean?

The yellow circle icon indicates how may people in your circle (people you follow) have left reviews on a dish.

The blue influencer badge indicates how many influencers have reviewed a dish. Influencers are granted their status by the Dished team. Influencers are our most active and influential users, professionals in the space, and our editors in each city. You can apply to become an influencer by editing your profile in the app.
